We empower students to reach their academic & social potential.

We want Haitian students to take advantage of the growing campaign in the use of Modern Technology in doing business to carve a niche for themselves and positively affecting their environment by training. This is as we also hope that the participants will also train and even employ others and as such cutting down the rate of unemployment in Haiti.

Your donation provides access to technology and Internet to Haitian students who might otherwise go without.
